Motivation: 空间转录组学(ST)能同时映射组织形态和空间分辨的基因表达，但需要开发计算方法直接从常规组织病理学图像预测空间通路活性，以研究肿瘤微环境异质性。

Method: 提出计算框架，使用计算病理学基础模型提取的图像特征，在55和100微米分辨率下，从H&E染色组织病理学图像预测空间通路活性。在三个独立的乳腺癌和肺癌ST数据集上评估，比较线性和非线性预测模型。

Abstract: Spatial transcriptomics (ST) enables simultaneous mapping of tissue morphology and spatially resolved gene expression, offering unique opportunities to study tumor microenvironment heterogeneity. Here, we introduce a computational framework that predicts spatial pathway activity directly from hematoxylin-and-eosin-stained histology images at microscale resolution 55 and 100 um. Using image features derived from a computational pathology foundation model, we found that TGFb signaling was the most accurately predicted pathway across three independent breast and lung cancer ST datasets. In 87-88% of reliably predicted cases, the resulting spatial TGFb activity maps reflected the expected contrast between tumor and adjacent non-tumor regions, consistent with the known role of TGFb in regulating interactions within the tumor microenvironment. Notably, linear and nonlinear predictive models performed similarly, suggesting that image features may relate to pathway activity in a predominantly linear fashion or that nonlinear structure is small relative to measurement noise. These findings demonstrate that features extracted from routine histopathology may recover spatially coherent and biologically interpretable pathway patterns, offering a scalable strategy for integrating image-based inference with ST information in tumor microenvironment studies.

### [2] [Understanding temperature tuning in energy-based models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.09152)
*Peter W Fields,Vudtiwat Ngampruetikorn,David J Schwab,Stephanie E Palmer*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 温度调节是生成模型中常见的启发式方法，用于平衡生成保真度与多样性。本文提出一个解释性框架，表明在具有大"能隙"的系统中，稀疏数据学习会导致模型高估高能态概率，而降低采样温度可以纠正这种偏差。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 生成复杂系统的模型通常需要事后参数调整才能产生有用输出，例如蛋白质设计的能量模型在人工低温下采样以生成新颖功能序列。这种温度调节是机器学习中常见但理解不足的启发式方法，用于控制生成保真度与多样性之间的权衡。

Method: 开发了一个可解释、物理动机的框架来分析温度调节现象。通过分析系统能量景观与数据规模之间的相互作用，表征了最优采样温度如何依赖于这些因素。

Result: 研究表明，在具有大"能隙"的系统中，稀疏数据学习会导致模型系统性地高估高能态概率，降低采样温度可以纠正这种偏差。更重要的是，发现降低采样温度并不总是可取的，在某些条件下提高温度反而能获得更好的生成性能。

Conclusion: 该框架将事后温度调节视为一种诊断工具，揭示了真实数据分布的特性和学习模型的局限性，为理解生成模型中的温度调节提供了理论基础。

Abstract: Generative models of complex systems often require post-hoc parameter adjustments to produce useful outputs. For example, energy-based models for protein design are sampled at an artificially low ''temperature'' to generate novel, functional sequences. This temperature tuning is a common yet poorly understood heuristic used across machine learning contexts to control the trade-off between generative fidelity and diversity. Here, we develop an interpretable, physically motivated framework to explain this phenomenon. We demonstrate that in systems with a large ''energy gap'' - separating a small fraction of meaningful states from a vast space of unrealistic states - learning from sparse data causes models to systematically overestimate high-energy state probabilities, a bias that lowering the sampling temperature corrects. More generally, we characterize how the optimal sampling temperature depends on the interplay between data size and the system's underlying energy landscape. Crucially, our results show that lowering the sampling temperature is not always desirable; we identify the conditions where \emph{raising} it results in better generative performance. Our framework thus casts post-hoc temperature tuning as a diagnostic tool that reveals properties of the true data distribution and the limits of the learned model.

### [3] [Riemannian vs. Euclidean Representation of Gait Kinematics: A Comparative Analysis](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.09158)
*Tomáš Bůžek*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 该研究提出了一种基于黎曼流形（SPD矩阵）的计算框架来分析步态运动，发现传统欧几里得度量显示步态变异性随速度线性增加，而黎曼度量揭示了"倒U型"非线性模式，表明高速时运动系统通过遵循最小努力测地线轨迹来优化效率。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统基于欧几里得几何的线性模型无法捕捉生物系统的内在非线性动力学和姿势依赖性，限制了复杂人体运动（如步态）的准确量化，这对于临床诊断和康复至关重要。

Method: 提出一个计算框架，将运动学数据映射到对称正定（SPD）矩阵的黎曼流形上。使用对数欧几里得度量将原始骨骼姿态序列转换为几何特征向量，以量化三种速度（慢、中、快）下的步态变异性和平滑度。

Result: 比较分析显示几何方法存在关键差异：欧几里得度量显示变异性随速度严格线性增加（慢<中<快），暗示不稳定性；而黎曼度量揭示了随速度变化的非线性"倒U型"模式，特别是在高速（冲刺）时观察到方差稳定化，表明运动系统通过遵循最小努力测地线轨迹来优化效率。

Conclusion: 基于流形的表示相比标准线性方法对生物力学效率具有更优的敏感性，为未来临床生物力学中的诊断算法和可解释机器学习模型提供了坚实基础。

Abstract: Accurate quantification of complex human movements, such as gait, is essential for clinical diagnosis and rehabilitation but is often limited by traditional linear models rooted in Euclidean geometry. These frameworks frequently fail to capture the intrinsic non-linear dynamics and posture-dependent dependencies of biological systems. To address this, we present a computational framework that maps kinematic data onto a Riemannian manifold of Symmetric Positive Definite (SPD) matrices. Using the Log-Euclidean metric, we transformed raw skeletal pose sequences into geometric feature vectors to quantify gait variability and smoothness across three velocity profiles: slow, medium, and fast. Our comparative analysis reveals a critical divergence between geometric approaches. While Euclidean metrics exhibit a strictly linear increase in variability with speed (Slow < Medium < Fast), implying instability, the proposed Riemannian metrics reveal a non-linear "inverted-U'' pattern with varying speeds. Specifically, we observed a stabilization of variance at high speeds (sprinting), suggesting that the motor system optimizes efficiency by adhering to geodesic trajectories of minimum effort. These findings demonstrate that manifold-based representations offer superior sensitivity to biomechanical efficiency compared to standard linear methods, providing a robust foundation for future diagnostic algorithms and explainable machine learning models in clinical biomechanics.

### [4] [MoDaH achieves rate optimal batch correction](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.09259)
*Yang Cao,Zongming Ma*

Main category: stat.ME

TL;DR: MoDaH是首个具有理论保证的批次效应校正方法，基于高斯混合模型框架，在单细胞组学数据中达到最小最大最优误差率，实验表现优于现有方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 单细胞组学数据分析中批次效应是重大挑战，现有计算方法虽然经验上成功但缺乏理论保证，无法评估其可靠性和泛化能力。

Method: 提出基于高斯混合模型的MoDaH算法，通过显式参数化批次效应，利用各向异性高斯混合聚类的最新理论进展实现批次校正。

Result: 建立了批次校正的最小最大最优误差率理论保证，并在多种单细胞RNA-seq和空间蛋白质组学数据集上验证，性能达到或超越现有最佳启发式方法。

Conclusion: MoDaH是首个具有理论保证的批次校正方法，在去除技术噪声和保留生物信号之间取得良好平衡，为可靠的单细胞数据分析提供了理论基础。

Abstract: Batch effects pose a significant challenge in the analysis of single-cell omics data, introducing technical artifacts that confound biological signals. While various computational methods have achieved empirical success in correcting these effects, they lack the formal theoretical guarantees required to assess their reliability and generalization. To bridge this gap, we introduce Mixture-Model-based Data Harmonization (MoDaH), a principled batch correction algorithm grounded in a rigorous statistical framework.
  Under a new Gaussian-mixture-model with explicit parametrization of batch effects, we establish the minimax optimal error rates for batch correction and prove that MoDaH achieves this rate by leveraging the recent theoretical advances in clustering data from anisotropic Gaussian mixtures. This constitutes, to the best of our knowledge, the first theoretical guarantee for batch correction. Extensive experiments on diverse single-cell RNA-seq and spatial proteomics datasets demonstrate that MoDaH not only attains theoretical optimality but also achieves empirical performance comparable to or even surpassing those of state-of-the-art heuristics (e.g., Harmony, Seurat-V5, and LIGER), effectively balancing the removal of technical noise with the conservation of biological signal.
