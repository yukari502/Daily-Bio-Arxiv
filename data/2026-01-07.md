### [1] [Apparent Selection Pressure for Channel Capacity in Bacterial Chemotactic Sensors](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.02446)
*Ziyi Cui,Sarah Marzen*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 通过异质MWC模型分析大肠杆菌Tar/Tsr化学受体簇的静态感知极限，发现通道容量在参数空间中呈现显著局部最大值，而有效希尔系数和动态范围未显示类似优化，表明个体细胞水平上可能选择通道容量优化。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究细菌趋化感知如何将噪声化学信号转化为奔跑和翻滚行为，特别关注大肠杆菌混合Tar/Tsr化学受体簇的静态感知极限，以理解个体细胞水平的感知优化机制。

Method: 使用异质Monod-Wyman-Changeux (MWC) 模型分析混合Tar/Tsr化学受体簇，通过扫描七维参数空间，计算三个感知性能指标：通道容量、有效希尔系数和动态范围。

Result: 在大肠杆菌样参数范围内，通道容量始终呈现显著的局部最大值，而有效希尔系数和动态范围未显示类似优化。实现最大通道容量的输入分布是双峰的，表明个体细胞通过采样低浓度和高浓度区域来最大化信息。

Conclusion: 这些结果表明，在个体细胞水平上，大肠杆菌受体簇可能选择优化通道容量，而非其他感知性能指标，这为理解细菌趋化感知的进化优化提供了新见解。

Abstract: Bacterial chemotactic sensing converts noisy chemical signals into running and tumbling. We analyze the static sensing limits of mixed Tar/Tsr chemoreceptor clusters in individual Escherichia coli cells using a heterogeneous Monod-Wyman-Changeux (MWC) model. By sweeping a seven-dimensional parameter space, we compute three sensing performance metrics-channel capacity, effective Hill coefficient, and dynamic range. Across E. coli-like parameter regimes, we consistently observe pronounced local maxima of channel capacity, whereas neither the effective Hill coefficient nor the dynamic range exhibit comparable optimization. The capacity-achieving input distribution is bimodal, which implies that individual cells maximize information by sampling both low- and high concentration regimes. Together, these results suggest that, at the individual-cell level, channel capacity may be selected for in E. coli receptor clusters.

### [2] [Simple chemical systems with chaos](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.02787)
*Tomislav Plesa,Julien Clinton Sprott*

Main category: nlin.CD

TL;DR: 本文发现了更简单的三维混沌化学动力系统，将所需单项式数量从9个减少到6-7个，反应数量从7个减少到4-5个，量化了混沌化学系统的结构复杂性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有简单混沌动力系统（5-6个单项式）都不是化学动力系统，而现有混沌化学系统需要至少9个单项式和7个反应，存在结构复杂性差距。

Method: 首先证明了混沌化学系统的基本性质（三维至少6个单项式，至少一个负二次项），然后利用这些性质通过计算搜索找到了20个更简单的混沌化学系统。

Result: 发现了20个三维混沌化学系统：6个单项式（最少4个二次项）或7个单项式（最少2个二次项），对应CRN只需4个反应（3个二次）或5个反应（2个二次）。

Conclusion: 混沌化学系统的结构复杂性比之前认为的要低，它们普遍存在，这为研究化学系统中的混沌现象提供了更简单的模型。

Abstract: A number of simple chaotic three-dimensional dynamical systems (DSs) with quadratic polynomials on the right-hand sides are reported in the literature, containing exactly 5 or 6 monomials of which only 1 or 2 are quadratic. However, none of these simple systems are chemical dynamical systems (CDSs) - a special subset of polynomial DSs that model the dynamics of mass-action chemical reaction networks (CRNs). In particular, only a small number of three-dimensional quadratic CDSs with chaos are reported, all of which have at least 9 monomials and at least 3 quadratics, with CRNs containing at least 7 reactions and at least 3 quadratic ones. To bridge this gap, in this paper we prove some basic properties of chaotic CDSs, including that those in three dimensions have at least 6 monomials, at least one of which is negative and quadratic. We then use these results to computationally find 20 chaotic three-dimensional CDSs with 6 monomials and as few as 4 quadratics, or 7 monomials and as few as 2 quadratics. At the CRN level, some of these systems have 4 reactions of which only 3 are quadratic, or 5 reactions with only 2 being quadratic. These results quantify structural complexity of chaotic CDSs, and indicate that they are ubiquitous.

### [3] [Multi-scale Graph Autoregressive Modeling: Molecular Property Prediction via Next Token Prediction](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.02530)
*Zhuoyang Jiang,Yaosen Min,Peiran Jin,Lei Chen*

Main category: cs.LG

TL;DR: CamS是一种将分子图转化为序列的表示方法，使解码器Transformer能通过标准的下一个token预测学习分子图，在分子性质预测任务上达到SOTA性能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: SMILES序列缺乏明确的拓扑结构信息，而图原生掩码建模虽然能捕捉连接性，但可能破坏关键的化学细节（如活性悬崖）。需要一种能同时保留拓扑结构和化学细节的表示方法。

Method: 1. 挖掘数据驱动的连接感知基序；2. 通过基于支架的广度优先搜索将基序序列化，建立从核心到外围的稳定顺序；3. 通过从细到粗的基序尺度拼接序列，实现分层建模。

Result: CamS-LLaMA在MoleculeNet和活性悬崖基准MoleculeACE上达到最先进的性能，优于基于SMILES的语言模型和强大的图基线模型。

Conclusion: CamS通过多尺度因果序列化有效驱动注意力关注决定活性悬崖的差异，为分子性质预测提供了既保留拓扑结构又保持化学细节的有效表示方法。

Abstract: We present Connection-Aware Motif Sequencing (CamS), a graph-to-sequence representation that enables decoder-only Transformers to learn molecular graphs via standard next-token prediction (NTP). For molecular property prediction, SMILES-based NTP scales well but lacks explicit topology, whereas graph-native masked modeling captures connectivity but risks disrupting the pivotal chemical details (e.g., activity cliffs). CamS bridges this gap by serializing molecular graphs into structure-rich causal sequences. CamS first mines data-driven connection-aware motifs. It then serializes motifs via scaffold-rooted breadth-first search (BFS) to establish a stable core-to-periphery order. Crucially, CamS enables hierarchical modeling by concatenating sequences from fine to coarse motif scales, allowing the model to condition global scaffolds on dense, uncorrupted local structural evidence. We instantiate CamS-LLaMA by pre-training a vanilla LLaMA backbone on CamS sequences. It achieves state-of-the-art performance on MoleculeNet and the activity-cliff benchmark MoleculeACE, outperforming both SMILES-based language models and strong graph baselines. Interpretability analysis confirms that our multi-scale causal serialization effectively drives attention toward cliff-determining differences.

</details>
