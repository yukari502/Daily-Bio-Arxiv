*Henrique RM Antoniolli,Lívia Kmetzsch,Charley C Staats*

Main category: q-bio.GN

TL;DR: FUNGAR是一个开源流程，用于直接从短读长测序数据中快速鉴定抗真菌耐药基因和突变，特别针对宏基因组样本。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Conclusion: FUNGAR提供了一个有效的解决方案，用于直接从测序读长中检测抗真菌耐药性，填补了真菌耐药性检测工具的空缺，特别适用于宏基因组分析场景。

Abstract: Motivation: Antifungal resistance has become an increasing global concern in both clinical and environmental health. FUNGAR employs translated alignments with DIAMOND and curated data from the FungAMR database to detect amino acid substitutions across all six open reading frames. ### [2] [Systematic Evaluation of Single-Cell Foundation Model Interpretability Reveals Attention Captures Co-Expression Rather Than Unique Regulatory Signal](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.17532)
*Ihor Kendiukhov*

Main category: q-bio.GN

TL;DR: 作者提出了一个系统评估框架（37种分析、153个统计测试、4种细胞类型、2种扰动模式），用于评估单细胞基础模型中的机制可解释性。应用该框架到scGPT和Geneformer发现：注意力模式编码了结构化的生物学信息，但这对扰动预测没有增量价值；简单的基因级基线模型表现更好；注意力-相关性关系具有上下文依赖性，但基因级优势是普遍的。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 当前单细胞基础模型（如scGPT和Geneformer）在机制可解释性方面缺乏系统评估标准。研究者需要了解这些模型的注意力机制是否真正编码了有意义的生物学信息，以及这些信息是否对下游任务（如扰动预测）有实际价值。

Method: 开发了一个包含37种分析、153个统计测试的系统评估框架，应用于4种细胞类型和2种扰动模式。使用该框架评估scGPT和Geneformer模型，分析注意力模式与生物学结构（如蛋白质-蛋白质相互作用、转录调控）的关系，并比较注意力边、相关性边与简单基因级基线在扰动预测任务上的表现。

2. 但这种结构对扰动预测没有增量价值：简单基因级基线（AUROC 0.81-0.88）显著优于注意力边和相关边（AUROC 0.70）
3. 成对边评分对预测没有贡献，因果消融调控头不会导致性能下降
4. 这些发现在K562和RPE1细胞中具有普遍性

Abstract: We present a systematic evaluation framework - thirty-seven analyses, 153 statistical tests, four cell types, two perturbation modalities - for assessing mechanistic interpretability in single-cell foundation models. Applying this framework to scGPT and Geneformer, we find that attention patterns encode structured biological information with layer-specific organisation - protein-protein interactions in early layers, transcriptional regulation in late layers - but this structure provides no incremental value for perturbation prediction: trivial gene-level baselines outperform both attention and correlation edges (AUROC 0.81-0.88 versus 0.70), pairwise edge scores add zero predictive contribution, and causal ablation of regulatory heads produces no degradation. These findings generalise from K562 to RPE1 cells; the attention-correlation relationship is context-dependent, but gene-level dominance is universal. Cell-State Stratified Interpretability (CSSI) addresses an attention-specific scaling failure, improving GRN recovery up to 1.85x. The framework establishes reusable quality-control standards for the field.

  <summary>Details</summary>
