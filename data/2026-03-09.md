### [1] [Machine Learning for analysis of Multiple Sclerosis cross-tissue bulk and single-cell transcriptomics data](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05572)
*Francesco Massafra,Samuele Punzo,Silvia Giulia Galfré,Alessandro Maglione,Simone Pernice,Stefano Forti,Simona Rolla,Marco Beccuti,Marinella Clerico,Corrado Priami,Alina Sîrbu*

Main category: q-bio.GN

TL;DR: 开发了一个端到端机器学习流程，整合转录组数据，使用XGBoost分类MS患者与健康对照，结合SHAP解释模型，识别关键基因和通路。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 多发性硬化症（MS）是一种中枢神经系统慢性自身免疫疾病，其分子机制尚未完全阐明。需要整合多组学数据并应用可解释AI来深入理解MS的发病机制。

Method: 开发端到端机器学习流程，整合外周血单核细胞和脑脊液的转录组数据（包括bulk微阵列和单细胞RNA测序）。经过预处理、批次校正和基因去聚类后，训练XGBoost分类器区分MS患者与健康对照。使用SHAP进行可解释性分析，识别关键基因，并与差异表达分析（DEA）比较。进一步通过相互作用网络和通路富集分析研究SHAP优先基因。

Result: 模型表现出色，特别是在CSF B细胞中（AUC=0.94）和微阵列数据中（AUC=0.86）。SHAP基因选择与经典DEA互补。跨多个数据集识别的基因簇突出了免疫激活、非经典免疫检查点（ITK、CLEC2D、KLRG1、CEACAM1）、核糖体和翻译程序、泛素-蛋白酶体调控、脂质运输以及EB病毒相关通路。

Conclusion: 这种整合性和可解释性框架提供了超越传统分析的互补见解，为MS发病机制提供了新的机制假说和潜在生物标志物。

Abstract: Multiple Sclerosis (MS) is a chronic autoimmune disease of the central nervous system whose molecular mechanisms remain incompletely understood. In this study, we developed an end-to-end machine learning pipeline to analyze transcriptomic data from peripheral blood mononuclear cells and cerebrospinal fluid, integrating both bulk microarray and single-cell RNA sequencing datasets (concentrating on CD4+ and B-cells). After rigorous preprocessing, batch correction, and gene declustering, XGBoost classifiers were trained to distinguish MS patients from healthy controls. Explainable AI tools, namely SHapley Additive exPlanations (SHAP), were employed to identify key genes driving classification, and results were compared with Differential Expression Analysis (DEA). SHAP-prioritized genes were further investigated through interaction networks and pathway enrichment analyses. The models achieved strong performance, particularly in CSF B-cells (AUC=0.94) and microarray (AUC=0.86). SHAP gene selection proved to be complementary to classical DEA. Gene clusters identified across multiple datasets highlighted immune activation, non-canonical immune checkpoints (ITK, CLEC2D, KLRG1, CEACAM1), ribosomal and translational programs, ubiquitin-proteasome regulation, lipid trafficking, and Epstein-Barr virus-related pathways. Our integrative and explainable framework reveals complementary insights beyond conventional analysis and provides novel mechanistic hypotheses and potential biomarkers for MS pathogenesis.

### [2] [In-batch Relational Features Enhance Precision in An Unsupervised Medical Anomaly Detection Task](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05534)
*P. Bilha Githinji,Xi Yuan,Ijaz Gul,Lian Zhang,Jinhao Xu,Zhenglin Chen,Peiwu Qin,Dongmei Yu*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 该论文提出了一种通过超图估计和图卷积增强自编码器潜在表示的方法，以更好地区分正常解剖变异与病理异常，在脑肿瘤MRI数据集上显著提升了异常检测性能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在无监督医学图像异常检测中，正常解剖变异常被误判为病理异常，导致大量假阳性。现有方法难以充分整合健康人群的正常变异信息。

Method: 通过批处理超图估计和共享权重的图卷积层，在CNN自编码器的潜在表示中融入正常队列的上下文相似性，生成群体感知的嵌入表示。

Result: 在异质性脑肿瘤2D MRI数据集上，该方法将健康与病理样本的可分离性显著提升，AUC-ROC达到0.90（绝对增益5.7%），平均精度绝对提升16%（0.78 AP），降低了假阳性率。

Conclusion: 通过超图增强的自编码器表示能有效整合健康变异信息，提高异常检测性能。小批量上下文的大小与性能正相关，为整合健康变异提供了可调节的机制。

Abstract: Confounding pathology with normal anatomical variation remains a significant challenge in unsupervised medical-image anomaly detection, resulting in numerous false positives. To enhance integration of healthy variation, we augment the latent representation of a CNN autoencoder with contextual similarities within a normal cohort through batch-wise hypergraph estimation and a shared-weights graph convolution layer, producing a population-aware embedding. On a heterogeneous brain-tumor dataset of 2D MRI scans, the method improves separability between healthy and pathological samples, achieving an AUC-ROC of 0.90 (95% CI 0.84-0.95, 5.7% absolute gain), and a 16% absolute improvement in average precision (0.78 AP, 95% CI 0.66-0.89), thereby lowering false-positive rates. Moreover, both anomaly detection and downstream tumor versus no-tumor classification performance improve with the size of the mini-batch context captured in the augmented representation, suggesting a tunable lever for integrating healthy variation.

### [3] [Privacy-Preserving Collaborative Medical Image Segmentation Using Latent Transform Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05541)

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 提出PPCMI-SF框架，通过密钥化潜在变换和服务器端统一映射网络，在保护隐私的同时实现多机构协作医学图像分割


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 医学图像分割需要多机构协作训练，但隐私法规、数据孤岛和数据可用性不均等问题限制了原始数据的共享，现有潜在空间协作方法在分割准确性和隐私保护方面仍有不足

Method: 结合跳跃连接自编码器与密钥化潜在变换，客户端使用正交混合和置换保护潜在特征，服务器端采用统一映射网络进行多尺度潜在到潜在转换

Result: 在四个数据集上均获得高Dice分数和改善的边界精度（更低的HD95和ASD），性能与隐私无关基线相当，同时具备对反转和成员推理攻击的强抵抗力，实现实时推理和低通信开销

Conclusion: PPCMI-SF框架证明在不损害数据隐私的前提下，可以在多机构设置中实现准确高效的医学图像分割

Abstract: Collaborative training across multiple institutions is becoming essential for building reliable medical image segmentation models. However, privacy regulations, data silos, and uneven data availability prevent hospitals from sharing raw scans or annotations, limiting the ability to train generalizable models. Latent-space collaboration frameworks such as privacy-segmentation framework (SF) offer a promising alternative, but such methods still face challenges in segmentation accuracy and vulnerability to latent inversion and membership-inference attacks. This work introduces a privacy-preserving collaborative medical image segmentation framework (PPCMI-SF) designed for heterogeneous medical datasets. The approach combines skip-connected autoencoders for images and masks with a keyed latent transform that applies client-specific orthogonal mixing and permutation to protect latent features before they are shared. A unified mapping network on the server-side performs multi-scale latent-to-latent translation, enabling segmentation inference without exposing raw data. Experiments on four datasets: PSFH ultrasound, ultrasound nerve segmentation, FUMPE CTA, and cardiac MRI show that the proposed PPCMI-SF consistently achieves high Dice scores and improved boundary accuracy, as reflected by lower 95th percentile Hausdorff distance (HD95) and average symmetric surface distance (ASD) compared to the current state-of-the-art and performs competitively with privacy-agnostic baselines. Privacy tests confirm strong resistance to inversion and membership attacks, and the overall system achieves real-time inference with low communication overhead. These results demonstrate that accurate and efficient medical image segmentation can be achieved without compromising data privacy in multi-institution settings.

### [4] [Multicellular Tumour Spheroids Exposure to Pulsed Electric Field: A Combined Experimental and Mathematical Modelling Study Highlighting Temporal Dynamics of DAMP Release and Accelerated Regrowth at Intermediate Field Intensities](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06087)

### [5] [A recipe for scalable attention-based MLIPs: unlocking long-range accuracy with all-to-all node attention](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06567)
*Eric Qu,Brandon M. Wood,Aditi S. Krishnapriyan,Zachary W. Ulissi*

Main category: cs.LG

TL;DR: AllScAIP是一种基于注意力机制、能量守恒的机器学习原子间势模型，通过全节点注意力组件处理长程相互作用，可扩展到1亿训练样本，在分子系统上达到SOTA精度。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有机器学习原子间势模型在处理大系统（如生物分子和电解质）时难以准确捕捉长程相互作用，通常需要依赖显式的物理基项或组件。

Method: 提出AllScAIP模型，采用基于注意力的能量守恒架构，通过数据驱动的全节点注意力组件处理长程相互作用，模型可扩展到1亿训练样本规模。

Result: 在分子系统上达到最先进的能量/力精度，在物理基评估（OMol25）中表现优异，在材料（OMat24）和催化剂（OC20）基准上具有竞争力，并能进行稳定长时间尺度的分子动力学模拟。

Conclusion: 研究表明：在小数据/小模型情况下，归纳偏置能提高样本效率；但随着数据和模型规模扩大，这些优势会减弱甚至逆转，而全节点注意力对于捕捉长程相互作用至关重要。

Abstract: Machine-learning interatomic potentials (MLIPs) have advanced rapidly, with many top models relying on strong physics-based inductive biases. However, as models scale to larger systems like biomolecules and electrolytes, they struggle to accurately capture long-range (LR) interactions, leading current approaches to rely on explicit physics-based terms or components. In this work, we propose AllScAIP, a straightforward, attention-based, and energy-conserving MLIP model that scales to O(100 million) training samples. It addresses the long-range challenge using an all-to-all node attention component that is data-driven. Extensive ablations reveal that in low-data/small-model regimes, inductive biases improve sample efficiency. However, as data and model size scale, these benefits diminish or even reverse, while all-to-all attention remains critical for capturing LR interactions. Our model achieves state-of-the-art energy/force accuracy on molecular systems, as well as a number of physics-based evaluations (OMol25), while being competitive on materials (OMat24) and catalysts (OC20). Furthermore, it enables stable, long-timescale MD simulations that accurately recover experimental observables, including density and heat of vaporization predictions.

</details>
