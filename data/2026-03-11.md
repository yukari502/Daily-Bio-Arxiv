### [1] [Exploring Strategies for Personalized Radiation Therapy Part IV: An Interaction-Picture Approach to Quantifying the Abscopal Effect](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.08751)
*Hao Peng,Laurentiu Pop,Kai Jiang,Faya Zhang,Debabrata Saha,Raquibul Hannan,Robert Timmerman*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 该研究引入量子力学中的相互作用图像变换来量化PULSAR放疗中的远隔效应，将其视为连续随机现象而非二元响应，并在两种小鼠肿瘤模型中验证了该方法。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统放疗中远隔效应存在争议且难以量化。PULSAR（个性化超分割立体定向自适应放疗）通过延长分次间隔可能增强系统性免疫激活，增加远隔效应发生概率，但需要新的量化方法来评估这种效应。

Method: 引入量子力学中的相互作用图像变换，分离肿瘤固有生长与放疗及免疫介导的扰动。在两种临床前双侧肿瘤模型（4T1和MC38）中测试该方法，量化原发与继发肿瘤间的相互作用强度。

Result: 该方法能够在个体水平上量化原发与继发肿瘤间的相互作用强度，捕捉随时间变化的动态过程，而非依赖队列平均值。将远隔效应框架化为连续随机现象。

Conclusion: 该框架为未来研究提供了灵活性，特别适用于PULSAR联合放疗和免疫治疗的研究，可比较不同放疗剂量和分次方案，并整合免疫检查点抑制剂。有助于跨研究比较远隔效应并标准化报告。

Abstract: We revisit the controversial "abscopal" effect in the context of Personalized Ultra-Fractionated Stereotactic Adaptive Radiotherapy (PULSAR). By allowing long interval between fractions, PULSAR may enhance systemic immune activation and increase the likelihood of abscopal responses compared with conventional daily fractionation. To quantify treatment-induced effects, we introduce an interaction-picture transformation adapted from quantum mechanics, which separates intrinsic tumor growth from radiation and immune-mediated perturbations. In this preliminary study, we tested this method to two preclinical bilateral tumor models (4T1 and MC38). Our model provides a quantitative measure of the interaction strength between primary and secondary tumors at the individual level, capturing dynamics over time rather than relying solely on cohort averages. This approach frames the abscopal effect as a continuous, stochastic phenomenon rather than a binary response. The framework is flexible for future studies, particularly in concurrent radiation and immunotherapy with PULSAR, where different radiation doses and fractionation schedules can be compared, and immune checkpoint inhibitors (ICIs) can be incorporated to further enhance systemic anti-tumor immunity. The framework can also help us make cross-study comparison of abscopal effects and standardizes the reporting of abscopal magnitude beyond simple statistical significance.

### [3] [Association of Radiologic PPFE Change with Mortality in Lung Cancer Screening Cohorts](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.09531)

Main category: q-bio.QM

However, the clinical significance of radiologic PPFE progression in lung cancer screening populations remains unclear.   Interpretation: Radiologic PPFE progression independently associates with mortality across two large lung cancer screening cohorts and with adverse clinical outcomes. Quantitative assessment of PPFE progression may provide a clinically relevant imaging biomarker for identifying individuals at increased respiratory risk within screening programmes.

*Alexandru Iosif*

Main category: q-bio.MN

TL;DR: 该论文认为质量作用网络中的守恒量与内部循环存在对偶关系，并提出了最大不变多面体支撑集与预簇之间的对偶猜想，以及虹吸与预簇的对偶关系猜想。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究质量作用网络（化学反应网络的特例）中的结构关系，探索守恒量、内部循环、不变多面体支撑集、预簇和虹吸之间的对偶关系，以深化对这类动力系统结构的理解。

Method: 引入最大不变多面体支撑集的概念，通过理论分析提出两个主要猜想：1）预簇与最大不变多面体支撑集之间存在对偶关系；2）虹吸与预簇是对偶对象（基于最大不变多面体支撑集与虹吸的密切关系）。

Result: 提出了两个重要的理论猜想：预簇与最大不变多面体支撑集的对偶关系，以及虹吸与预簇的对偶关系。这些猜想为理解质量作用网络的结构特性提供了新的理论框架。

Conclusion: 质量作用网络中的守恒量与内部循环存在对偶关系，最大不变多面体支撑集、预簇和虹吸之间可能存在系统的对偶结构，这为进一步研究化学反应网络的动力学特性提供了新的理论方向。

Given the close relation between maximal invariant polyhedral supports and siphons, we also conjecture that siphons and preclusters are dual objects.

### [5] [Automated Classification of Homeostasis Structure in Input-Output Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.08882)
*Xinni Lin,Fernando Antoneli,Yangyang Wang*

Main category: q-bio.MN

TL;DR: 开发Python算法自动从网络拓扑识别稳态子网络及其条件，扩展理论框架至多输入节点网络，提供可扩展的稳态机制分类方法


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 生物系统中稳态现象普遍存在，但现有数学方法依赖组合枚举和图论概念，随着网络规模增大会变得难以处理，限制了在生物应用中的可访问性和实用性

Method: 开发基于Python的算法，仅需网络连接结构和指定的输入输出节点，自动识别相关图论结构并枚举所有稳态机制；将理论框架从单输入单输出网络扩展到多输入节点网络，通过增强的单输入节点表示实现

Result: 算法在多种生物示例中展示适用性，包括小型和大型网络、单或多输入节点/参数网络，以及输入输出重合的情况；提供可扩展的系统性稳态机制分类方法

Conclusion: 该计算框架为复杂生物网络中的稳态机制分类提供了可扩展和系统性的方法，促进了先进数学理论在广泛生物系统中的应用

Abstract: Homeostasis is widely observed in biological systems and refers to their ability to maintain an output quantity approximately constant despite variations in external disturbances. Mathematically, homeostasis can be formulated through an input-output function mapping an external parameter to an output variable. Infinitesimal homeostasis occurs at isolated points where the derivative of this input-output function vanishes, allowing tools from singularity theory and combinatorial matrix theory to characterize homeostatic mechanisms in terms of network topology. However, the required combinatorial enumeration becomes increasingly intractable as network size grows, and the reliance on advanced graph-theoretic concepts limits accessibility and practical use in biological applications. To overcome these limitations, we develop a Python-based algorithm that automates the identification of homeostasis subnetworks and their associated homeostasis conditions directly from network topology. Given an input-output network specified solely by its connectivity structure and designated input and output nodes, the algorithm identifies the relevant graph-theoretical structures and enumerates all homeostatic mechanisms. We demonstrate its applicability across a range of biological examples, including small and large networks, networks with single or multiple input nodes or parameters, and cases where input and output coincide. This wide applicability stems from our extension of the theoretical framework from single-input-single-output networks to networks with multiple input nodes through an augmented single-input-node representation. The resulting computational framework provides a scalable and systematic approach to classifying homeostatic mechanisms in complex biological networks, facilitating the application of advanced mathematical theory to a broad range of biological systems.
