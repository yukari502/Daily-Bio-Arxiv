### [2] [SHREC: A Spectral Embedding-Based Approach for Ab-Initio Reconstruction of Helical Molecules](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12307)

TL;DR: SHREC算法直接从二维冷冻电镜图像恢复螺旋片段的投影角度，无需先验螺旋对称参数，通过谱嵌入技术实现自动螺旋重建。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统螺旋重建方法需要准确的初始螺旋对称参数估计，通常通过试错或先验知识获得，这可能导致错误重建，限制了从头螺旋重建的可靠性。

Method: SHREC算法利用螺旋片段投影形成一维流形的洞察，使用谱嵌入技术直接从二维冷冻电镜图像恢复投影角度，无需螺旋对称参数先验知识。

Result: 在公开数据集上的实验验证表明，SHREC能够实现高分辨率重建，同时准确恢复螺旋参数，仅需知道样品的轴向对称群信息。

However, reconstructing helical assemblies presents unique challenges due to their inherent symmetry and the need to determine unknown helical symmetry parameters. These requirements can lead to incorrect reconstructions, limiting the reliability of ab initio helical reconstruction.
  In this work, we present SHREC (Spectral Helical REConstruction), an algorithm that directly recovers the projection angles of helical segments from their two-dimensional cryo-EM images, without requiring prior knowledge of helical symmetry parameters. Experimental validation on publicly available datasets demonstrates that SHREC achieves high resolution reconstructions while accurately recovering helical parameters, requiring only knowledge of the specimen's axial symmetry group. By eliminating the need for initial symmetry estimates, SHREC offers a more robust and automated pathway for determining helical structures in cryo-EM.
