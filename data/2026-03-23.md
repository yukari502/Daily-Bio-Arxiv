### [1] [Mathematical Modeling of Cancer-Bacterial Therapy: Analysis and Numerical Simulation via Physics-Informed Neural Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.19326)
*Ayoub Farkane,David Lassounon*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 本文提出一个数学模型研究细菌癌症疗法，通过五维非线性反应扩散方程描述肿瘤生长、细菌定植、氧气水平、免疫抑制细胞因子和细菌通讯的相互作用，并利用物理信息神经网络求解系统。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 细菌癌症疗法利用厌氧细菌靶向肿瘤缺氧区域，但肿瘤生长、细菌定植、氧气水平、免疫抑制细胞因子和细菌通讯之间的相互作用缺乏量化研究，需要建立数学模型来理解这些复杂关系。

Method: 建立二维组织域中的五个耦合非线性反应扩散方程模型，证明模型的全局适定性并分析稳态稳定性。采用物理信息神经网络（PINN）进行无网格求解，结合残差稳定性和Sobolev近似误差界提供收敛保证。

Result: 获得整体误差率为O(n^-2 ln^4(n) + N^-1/2)，其中n为网络宽度，N为配置点数量。数值实验表明，长期治疗效果可能需要维持肿瘤缺氧区域，或使用更能耐受氧气的细菌才能实现持久的肿瘤控制。

Conclusion: 该数学模型成功量化了细菌癌症疗法中的关键相互作用，物理信息神经网络为复杂生物系统提供了有效的求解方法。研究结果为优化细菌癌症疗法提供了理论指导，强调了维持缺氧环境或使用耐氧细菌的重要性。

Abstract: Bacterial cancer therapy exploits anaerobic bacteria's ability to target hypoxia tumor regions, yet the interactions among tumor growth, bacterial colonization, oxygen levels, immunosuppressive cytokines, and bacterial communication remain poorly quantified. We present a mathematical model of five coupled nonlinear reaction-diffusion equations in a two-dimensional tissue domain. We proved the global well-posedness of the model and identified its steady states to analyze stability. Furthermore, a physics-informed neural network (PINN) solves the system without a mesh and without requiring extensive data. It provides convergence guarantees by combining residual stability and Sobolev approximation error bounds. This results in an overall error rate of O(n^-2 ln^4(n) + N^-1/2), which depends on the network width n and the number of collocation points N. We conducted several numerical experiments, including predicting the tumor's response to therapy. We also performed a sensitivity analysis of certain parameters. The results suggest that long-term therapeutic efficacy may require the maintenance of hypoxia regions in the tumor, or using bacteria that tolerate oxygen better, may be necessary for long-lasting tumor control.

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有的随机注意力蛋白质序列生成方法将所有存储序列同等对待，无法针对特定功能子集进行定向生成，需要一种无需重新训练就能实现条件生成的方法

Method: 在采样器的注意力logits中添加单个标量参数作为偏置，通过用户提供的少量序列（如结合筛选命中）和多重比参数控制生成偏向目标子集的程度，无需改变模型架构或重新训练

Result: 方法在五个Pfam家族（Kunitz、SH3、WW、Homeobox和Forkhead结构域）验证有效，应用于靶向疼痛信号钙通道的ω-芋螺毒素肽，从23个已知结合剂生成上千个候选序列，保持主要药效团和所有实验确定的结合决定簇

We term this discrepancy the calibration gap and show that it is predicted by a simple geometric measure of how well the encoding separates the functional subset from the rest of the family. Experiments on five Pfam families (Kunitz, SH3, WW, Homeobox, and Forkhead domains) confirm the monotonic relationship between separation and gap across a fourfold range of geometries. Applied to omega-conotoxin peptides targeting a calcium channel involved in pain signaling, curated seeding from 23 characterized binders produces over a thousand candidates that preserve the primary pharmacophore and all experimentally identified binding determinants. These results show that stochastic attention enables practitioners to expand a handful of experimentally characterized sequences into diverse candidate libraries without retraining a generative model.

### [4] [Stochastic Averaging and Statistical Inference of Glycolytic Pathway](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.19577)

TL;DR: 从随机生化反应网络出发，通过多尺度连续时间马尔可夫链模型，严格推导出糖酵解途径的简化ODE模型，并证明仅观测慢变量时的参数估计具有统计一致性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 糖酵解振荡等生物过程需要低维ODE模型来分析核心机制，但现有简化模型多从确定性描述出发，而非更准确反映离散随机反应事件的随机动力学。需要建立从随机公式推导简化模型的严格概率框架。

Method: 将完整系统建模为具有不同时间和丰度尺度的多尺度连续时间马尔可夫链。在适当的缩放机制和特定结构条件下，证明慢分量的动力学可由二维ODE近似。针对仅观测慢物种（果糖-6-磷酸和ADP）的情况，构建可处理的损失函数进行参数估计。

Abstract: Many biological processes exhibit oscillatory behavior. Among these, glycolytic oscillations have been extensively studied due to their well-characterized biochemical reaction networks. However, the complexity of these networks necessitates low-dimensional ordinary differential equation (ODE) models to identify core mechanisms and perform stability analysis. While previous studies proposed reduced ODE models, these were typically introduced from deterministic descriptions rather than the underlying stochastic dynamics, which more accurately represent discrete reaction events occurring at random times. In this paper, we develop a rigorous probabilistic framework for deriving a reduced Othmer-Aldridge model of the glycolytic pathway from its stochastic formulation. The full system is modeled as a multiscale continuous-time Markov chain with different time and abundance scales. Under an appropriate scaling regime and specific structural conditions, we prove that the dynamics of the slow components are approximated by a two-dimensional ODE. The proof is technically involved due to the network's complexity and strong coupling between its components. We further consider the problem of parameter estimation when observations are limited to the slow species: fructose-6-phosphate and ADP. The reduced system yields a tractable loss function depending solely on these variables. We prove that the resulting estimators are statistically consistent when the data originate from the full stochastic reaction network. Together, these results provide a mathematically rigorous framework linking stochastic biochemical reaction networks, reduced deterministic dynamics, and statistically reliable parameter estimation.
