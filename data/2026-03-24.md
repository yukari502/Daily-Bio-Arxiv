### [1] [CERN: Correcting Errors in Raw Nanopore Signals Using Hidden Markov Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.20420)
*Simon Ambrozak,Ulysse McConnell,Bhargav Srinivasan,Burak Ozkan,Can Firtina*

Main category: q-bio.GN

TL;DR: CERN是一种基于隐马尔可夫模型的纳米孔原始信号纠错方法，能提高原始信号分析的准确性，无需显著计算开销。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 纳米孔测序产生的原始电信号常包含噪声和处理错误，限制了原始信号分析的准确性。现有方法虽然能直接分析原始信号（无需碱基识别），但信号错误问题仍未解决。

Method: 提出CERN机制，训练并使用隐马尔可夫模型（HMM）来准确纠正信号错误。该方法能检测并修正原始信号中的错误。

Result: 在大肠杆菌、果蝇和人类基因组等多种数据集上的评估表明：1）持续提高底层原始信号分析工具的映射准确性；2）最小化新纳米孔化学下分割算法优化的负担；3）运行时不造成显著计算开销。

Conclusion: CERN提供了一种有效机制，能在进一步分析前系统识别和纠正纳米孔原始信号中的错误，为专门针对原始纳米孔信号设计的新型纠错机制奠定了基础。

Abstract: Nanopore sequencing can read substantially longer sequences of nucleic acid molecules than other sequencing methods, which has led to advances in genomic analysis such as the gapless human genome assembly. By analyzing the raw electrical signal reads that nanopore sequencing generates from molecules, existing works can map these reads without translating them into DNA characters (i.e., basecalling), allowing for quick and efficient analysis of sequencing data. However, raw signals often contain errors due to noise and mistakes when processing them, which limits the overall accuracy of raw signal analysis.
  Our goal in this work is to detect and correct errors in raw signals to improve the accuracy of raw signal analyses. To this end, we propose CERN, a mechanism that trains and utilizes a Hidden Markov Model (HMM) to accurately correct signal errors. Our extensive evaluation on various datasets including E. coli, Fruit Fly, and Human genomes shows that CERN 1) consistently improves the overall mapping accuracy of the underlying raw signal analysis tools, 2) minimizes the burden on segmentation algorithm optimization with newer nanopore chemistries, and 3) functions without causing substantial computational overhead. We conclude that CERN provides an effective mechanism to systematically identify and correct the errors in raw nanopore signals before further analysis, which can enable the development of a new class of error correction mechanisms purely designed for raw nanopore signals.
  CERN is available at https://github.com/STORMgroup/CERN. We also provide the scripts to fully reproduce our results on our GitHub page.

### [2] [Towards Improved Short-term Hypoglycemia Prediction and Diabetes Management based on Refined Heart Rate Data](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.20345)
*Vaibhav Gupta,Florian Grensing,Beyza Cinar,Louisa van den Boom,Maria Maleshkova*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 提出两种新型心率数据插补方法CRBC和CMPV，用于糖尿病低血糖预测中缺失心率值的填补，CMPV在综合评估中表现最佳。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 低血糖是1型糖尿病患者的严重并发症，难以预测。现有预测方法依赖血糖和心率数据，但可穿戴传感器采集的心率数据常有缺失，需要有效的插补技术来提高预测准确性。

Method: 提出两种新型短期心率数据插补方法：控制加权有理贝塞尔曲线(CRBC)和控制分段三次埃尔米特插值多项式与映射峰谷控制点(CMPV)。使用两种数据模式捕捉指标，并结合RMSE分数创建综合评估指标来全面评估插补技术。

Result: 根据综合指标评估，CMPV在所有时间间隔上平均得分为0.33，表现最佳；CRBC得分为0.48。两种方法都能有效填补缺失心率值，CMPV优于现有替代方法。

Conclusion: 提出的CRBC和CMPV插补方法能准确填补缺失心率数据，有助于检测异常生理信号，实现早期预防措施，提高低血糖预测的准确性。

Abstract: Hypoglycemia is a severe condition of decreased blood glucose, specifically below 70 mg/dL (3.9 mmol/L). This condition can often be asymptomatic and challenging to predict in individuals with type 1 diabetes (T1D). Research on hypoglycemic prediction typically uses a combination of blood glucose readings and heart rate data to predict hypoglycemic events. Given that these features are collected through wearable sensors, they can sometimes have missing values, necessitating efficient imputation methods. This work makes significant contributions to the current state of the art by introducing two novel imputation techniques for imputing heart rate values over short-term horizons: Controlled Weighted Rational Bézier Curves (CRBC) and Controlled Piecewise Cubic Hermite Interpolating Polynomial with mapped peaks and valleys of Control Points (CMPV). In addition to these imputation methods, we employ two metrics to capture data patterns, alongside a combined metric that integrates the strengths of both individual metrics with RMSE scores for a comprehensive evaluation of the imputation techniques. According to our combined metric assessment, CMPV outperforms the alternatives with an average score of 0.33 across all time gaps, while CRBC follows with a score of 0.48. These findings clearly demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed imputation methods in accurately filling in missing heart rate values. Moreover, this study facilitates the detection of abnormal physiological signals, enabling the implementation of early preventive measures for more accurate diagnosis.

### [3] [Characterizing Long-Range Dependencies in Knee Joint Contact Mechanics: A Comparison of Topology Diffusion, Global Routing, and Hybrid Graph Neural Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.21020)
*Zhengye Pan,Jianwei Zuo,Jiajia Luo*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 比较拓扑扩散、全局路由及其混合方法在膝关节接触力学代理建模中的效果，混合模型表现最佳


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 有限元分析膝关节接触力学计算成本高，需要开发图神经网络代理模型，但有效表示关节力学响应中的长程依赖关系仍具挑战

Method: 系统比较拓扑扩散、全局路由及其混合方法，使用9名足球运动员变向动作的运动学和力数据生成图结构样本，在分组三折交叉受试者评估框架下比较五种架构

Result: 混合模型表现最佳，全场误差和峰值应力误差最低，高风险区域空间一致性最高；非混合模型中，标准拓扑扩散模型表现最好，仅路由策略效果较差

Conclusion: 拓扑扩散为膝关节接触力学代理建模提供了稳健基础，而添加全局路由可进一步改善临床相关高应力模式的重建

Abstract: Finite element analysis of knee joint contact mechanics is computationally expensive, which has motivated the development of graph neural network surrogate models. However, effectively representing long-range dependencies in joint mechanical responses remains challenging. This study systematically compared topology diffusion, global routing, and their hybridization for surrogate modeling of knee joint contact mechanics. Using kinematic and force data from nine soccer players performing change-of-direction maneuvers, finite element simulations were used to generate graph-structured samples for training and evaluation under a grouped three-fold cross-subject evaluation framework. Five architectures were compared: standard MeshGraphNet, hierarchical MeshGraphNet, a routing-only transformer, a topology-biased routing transformer, and a hybrid model. The hybrid model achieved the best overall performance, yielding the lowest full-field error and peak stress error, together with the highest spatial agreement for high-risk regions. Among the non-hybrid models, the standard topology-diffusion model performed best overall, whereas routing-only strategies were less effective. These findings indicate that topology diffusion provides a robust basis for surrogate modeling of knee joint contact mechanics within the present benchmark, while the addition of global routing can further improve reconstruction of clinically relevant high-stress patterns.

### [4] [GIP-RAG: An Evidence-Grounded Retrieval-Augmented Framework for Interpretable Gene Interaction and Pathway Impact Analysis](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.20321)
*Fujian Jia,Jiwen Gu,Cheng Lu,Dezhi Zhao,Mengjiang Huang,Yuanzhi Lu,Xin Liu,Kang Liu*

Main category: q-bio.MN

TL;DR: GIP-RAG是一个结合生物医学知识图谱与大语言模型的框架，用于推断和解释基因相互作用，支持多步骤推理和通路级功能影响分析。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 尽管有大量分子相互作用和通路数据，但整合异质知识源并在生物网络中实现可解释的多步骤推理仍然具有挑战性，这阻碍了对疾病机制的理解和精准医学的发展。

Method: 构建统一的基因相互作用知识图谱（整合KEGG、WikiPathways、SIGNOR、Pathway Commons和PubChem数据），通过查询驱动模块检索相关子图，结合结构化提示指导LLM进行逐步推理，识别直接和间接调控关系，并包含通路级功能影响模块模拟基因扰动传播。

Result: 该框架在多种生物场景下评估，能够生成一致、可解释且有证据支持的基因调控机制见解，支持对复杂分子系统的机制推理。

Conclusion: GIP-RAG提供了一个通用且可解释的方法，将知识图谱与检索增强的LLMs相结合，支持复杂分子系统中的机制推理。

Abstract: Understanding mechanistic relationships among genes and their impacts on biological pathways is essential for elucidating disease mechanisms and advancing precision medicine. Despite the availability of extensive molecular interaction and pathway data in public databases, integrating heterogeneous knowledge sources and enabling interpretable multi-step reasoning across biological networks remain challenging.
  We present GIP-RAG (Gene Interaction Prediction through Retrieval-Augmented Generation), a computational framework that combines biomedical knowledge graphs with large language models (LLMs) to infer and interpret gene interactions. The framework constructs a unified gene interaction knowledge graph by integrating curated data from KEGG, WikiPathways, SIGNOR, Pathway Commons, and PubChem. Given user-specified genes, a query-driven module retrieves relevant subgraphs, which are incorporated into structured prompts to guide LLM-based stepwise reasoning. This enables identification of direct and indirect regulatory relationships and generation of mechanistic explanations supported by biological evidence.
  Beyond pairwise interactions, GIP-RAG includes a pathway-level functional impact module that simulates propagation of gene perturbations through signaling networks and evaluates potential pathway state changes. Evaluation across diverse biological scenarios demonstrates that the framework generates consistent, interpretable, and evidence-supported insights into gene regulatory mechanisms.
  Overall, GIP-RAG provides a general and interpretable approach for integrating knowledge graphs with retrieval-augmented LLMs to support mechanistic reasoning in complex molecular systems.

### [5] [CellFluxRL: Biologically-Constrained Virtual Cell Modeling via Reinforcement Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.21743)
*Dongxia Wu,Shiye Su,Yuhui Zhang,Elaine Sui,Emma Lundberg,Emily B. Fox,Serena Yeung-Levy*

Main category: cs.LG

TL;DR: 使用强化学习对虚拟细胞生成模型进行后训练，通过生物学评估器作为奖励函数，提升细胞图像的生物学合理性


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有基于图像的虚拟细胞生成方法可能产生违反基本物理和生物学约束的不合理细胞图像，需要开发能生成"生物学有意义"而非仅"视觉真实"的虚拟细胞模型

Method: 提出用强化学习对最先进的CellFlux模型进行后训练，设计了涵盖生物功能、结构有效性和形态正确性三个类别的七个奖励函数，优化得到CellFluxRL模型

Result: CellFluxRL在所有奖励指标上均优于原始CellFlux模型，测试时缩放进一步提升了性能，实现了从"视觉真实"到"生物学有意义"的虚拟细胞生成

Conclusion: 通过强化学习强制执行基于物理的约束，提出了一个能够生成生物学合理虚拟细胞的建模框架，为加速药物发现提供了更可靠的工具

Abstract: Building virtual cells with generative models to simulate cellular behavior in silico is emerging as a promising paradigm for accelerating drug discovery. However, prior image-based generative approaches can produce implausible cell images that violate basic physical and biological constraints. To address this, we propose to post-train virtual cell models with reinforcement learning (RL), leveraging biologically meaningful evaluators as reward functions. We design seven rewards spanning three categories-biological function, structural validity, and morphological correctness-and optimize the state-of-the-art CellFlux model to yield CellFluxRL. CellFluxRL consistently improves over CellFlux across all rewards, with further performance boosts from test-time scaling. Overall, our results present a virtual cell modeling framework that enforces physically-based constraints through RL, advancing beyond "visually realistic" generations towards "biologically meaningful" ones.
